About Kristin Wolf

A Witness. A Teacher. A Builder of New Earth.

She came here to witness. She stayed to build. She writes what she lived, teaches what she survived, and holds space for people who are ready to stop pretending the old world was ever going to save them.

Kristin Wolf is a channeler, author, teacher, and crystal energy healing master based in Zimmerman, Minnesota. She spent decades leading people and developing leaders — and for the last decade has been pursuing the healing arts with the same intensity. She sits with people in the hardest moments of their lives, reads what their soul came here to do, and tells them the truth about what she sees.

She works with people one on one through the SOUL Blueprint — a six-month soul cartography journey built from your birth chart, written specifically for you. She runs year-long containers through the Spark. She leads retreats on sacred land. She runs a crystal shop out of her home in Zimmerman. And she writes — books, transmissions, witness work — because some things need to be said out loud.

Her work is rooted in lived experience. She is a grandmother, a survivor, a political activist, and a teacher who did not come to repeat what she read. She came to say what she survived, remembered, and was sent to say. It's all in her Soul Blueprint.
